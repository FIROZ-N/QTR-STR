Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver daily from 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM. You can select your preferred time slot during checkout.

Do you deliver outside Doha?

Yes, we deliver to all major cities in Qatar. Delivery charges may vary based on location.

What is your return policy?

You can return most items within 7 days of delivery. Perishable items have different return conditions.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track it from your account.
