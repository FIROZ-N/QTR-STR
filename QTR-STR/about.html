About Qatar Hypermarket

Since 2005, we've been committed to providing the finest shopping experience in Qatar, combining traditional values with modern convenience.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Qatar Hypermarket began as a small local grocery store with a vision to revolutionize the shopping experience in Qatar. Over the years, we've grown into one of the largest retail chains in the country, serving thousands of satisfied customers daily.

Our commitment to quality, customer service, and innovation has made us a trusted household name. We source the finest products from around the world while supporting local producers and businesses.
